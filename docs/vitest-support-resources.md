## How to Create a New Topic

### Step-by-Step Guide

1. **Click "+ New Topic"** button on the top right of the forum page
2. **Type your title** in the title field - Be clear and specific about your question or issue
3. **Select the category** from the dropdown menu:
   - Choose "Bugs" for bug reports
   - Choose "Help & Support" for general assistance
   - Choose "Feedback" for suggestions and feature requests
4. **Add optional tags** (if needed) - Click the "+" button to add relevant tags
5. **Write your content** in the body editor:
   - Use the formatting toolbar to add bold, italic, code blocks, links, images, etc.
   - Include reproduction steps for bugs
   - Add screenshots or error messages
   - Provide relevant details about your environment
6. **Click "Create Topic"** button to publish your post

### Before Posting

1. **Search the forum** for similar questions - your issue may already be solved
2. **Check the documentation** for guides and tutorials
3. **Review FAQs** for common questions and answers

### Tips for Quality Posts

1. **Choose the right category** - bugs in Bugs, general help in Support
2. **Write a clear title** - Be specific about your issue or question
3. **Provide details** - Include what you tried, error messages, screenshots, and your environment
4. **Be specific** - The more details you provide, the faster we can help

### Response Times

- **Community members:** Typically respond within a few hours
- **Support team:** 24-48 hours on weekdays
- **Critical issues:** Prioritized for faster response
- **Security issues:** Within 24 hours for initial response
