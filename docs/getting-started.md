## **Sign Up**

Getting started with CoreFix is quick and easy. Follow these steps to create your account and begin securing your cloud infrastructure.

---

## **Create Your Account**

### **Step 1: Registration**

* Visit the Sign Up page: [https://app.corefix.dev/signup](https://app.corefix.dev/signup)
* Enter your **email address**
* Create a secure password *(minimum 8 characters with uppercase, lowercase, and numbers)*
* Select your **region** *(Currently, only India is supported)*
* Accept the **Terms of Service** and **Privacy Policy**
* Click **Create Account**

---

### **Step 2: Approval Process**

* After registration, your account will be **reviewed by the CoreFix team**
* You will receive an email once your account is **approved**
* Only approved users can access the platform

---

### **Step 3: Email Verification & Onboarding**

* Check your inbox for a verification email
* Click the verification link
* You will be redirected to a **quick onboarding page**

On the onboarding page, you can:

* Provide **basic profile details**
* Select your **interests and features to explore**

**Note:**

* Completing onboarding is optional
* You can skip and proceed directly to the Home Dashboard

---

## **Account Types & Access Rules**

### **Business Email Users**

If you sign up using a **business email (company domain)**:

* An organization is automatically created
* You will be assigned as the Admin
* Users with the same domain or subdomain will be automatically added as collaborators

#### **Free Trial Benefits**

* 14-day free trial
* 20 AI usage credits included
* Access to core platform features including scans and insights

#### **After Trial Expiry**

Once either condition is met:

* 14 days are completed, or
* 20 AI credits are exhausted

Then:

* New scans cannot be created or launched
* Previously completed scan data will remain accessible
* Platform access becomes limited until upgrade

To continue usage, you must upgrade your plan

---

### **Personal Email Users**

If you sign up using a **personal email (e.g., Gmail, Yahoo)**:

* No organization will be created
* You cannot launch scans
* You are not eligible for the free trial

Access is limited to:

* Viewing the platform (restricted mode)

You can still:

* Be invited as a collaborator or external user by an organization
